About Us

What is CouGramTion?

CouGramTion is an innovative and intelligent course recommendation system designed to revolutionize the way students plan their academic journeys. Guided by cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, CouGramTion analyzes diverse data points, including academic performance and personal attributes, to predict and recommend tailored courses for incoming first-year students at Western Mindanao State University.

How it works?

By seamlessly integrating student data and advanced algorithms, CouGramTion accurately recommends the ideal courses for each student. Students simply sign up, provide their information, and CouGramTion does the rest. With CouGramTion, students can confidently embark on their educational paths, equipped with personalized course recommendations that align with their interests, strengths, and aspirations.
